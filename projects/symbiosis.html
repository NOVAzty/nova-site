Symbiosis (2024)

Video · Installation

Video Installation
About the Project

Symbiosis is a video installation centered around the theme of nuclear wastewater. Through the interplay of light and moving image, the work conjures a suspended, relic-like figure — a mutated entity born from environmental contamination. Its twisted form and sharp, monstrous contours are made all the more uncanny under shifting illumination, evoking a sense of unease and prompting reflection on the dangers of nuclear pollution.

By invoking sacred visual language and atmospheric tension, the work seeks to raise awareness about environmental degradation and to advocate for a renewed vision of coexistence between humanity and the natural world.
