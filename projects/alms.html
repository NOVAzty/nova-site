About the Project

Alms Giving to the Sky is centered around a traditional Tibetan funerary practice — sky burial.

By constructing an abstracted sky burial platform, and incorporating motifs of rotation, repetition, and ambient-noise-driven sonic experimentation, the piece blends emotional perception into a dense, immersive experience.

It attempts to evoke a state of individual dissolution — merging into the collective unconscious — and gestures toward a unique vision of communion between the human and the celestial.
